Hydrophobic VAE Powder repels water, making it ideal for moisture-sensitive applications. Waterproof VAE Powder offers enhanced water resistance, suitable for environments requiring durability against water exposure. Ordinary VAE Powder is versatile, used in general-purpose applications without specialized water resistance. Each type caters to distinct needs, with hydrophobic and waterproof variants addressing specific performance requirements, while ordinary powder serves broader, less demanding uses.</p></p>
