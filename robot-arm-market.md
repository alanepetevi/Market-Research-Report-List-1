<p><p>The robot arm market encompasses two main types: traditional robot arms and collaborative robot arms. Traditional robot arms are industrial-grade machines designed for high-speed, precision tasks in manufacturing environments, often requiring safety cages due to their powerful movements. In contrast, collaborative robot arms, or cobots, are designed to work alongside humans, featuring advanced sensors and safety features that enable safe interaction. This flexibility allows them to handle diverse applications, from assembly to packaging, while enhancing workplace efficiency and safety.</p></p>
