<p><ul><li>MPP</li><li>Sendust</li><li>High Flux</li><li>Fe-Si</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft magnetic powder core market encompasses several types, each tailored for specific applications. MPP (Modified Powdered Iron) cores offer low core losses and high saturation flux density, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Sendust cores, composed of iron, silicon, and aluminum, excel in high-frequency and high-temperature environments. High Flux cores provide enhanced permeability and lower losses, suitable for power electronics. Fe-Si (Iron-Silicon) cores are known for their excellent magnetic properties in various industrial applications. "Others" categorically includes emerging materials or specialized blends.</p></p>
