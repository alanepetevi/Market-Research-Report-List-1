<p><ul><li>Atmosphere Furnaces</li><li>Vacuum Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat furnace market is segmented into atmosphere furnaces and vacuum furnaces. Atmosphere furnaces utilize controlled atmospheres to prevent oxidation during heating, making them ideal for processes like annealing and hardening. In contrast, vacuum furnaces operate under low-pressure environments, eliminating the risk of contamination from air, which enhances material integrity. Both types cater to various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics, focusing on improving the quality and performance of heat-treated components.</p></p>
