<p><ul><li>Electronic Scanning</li><li>Sector Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing (PAUT) machine market is characterized by two primary types: electronic scanning and sector scanning. </p><p>Electronic scanning utilizes electronically controlled piezoelectric elements, enabling rapid and precise inspection by adjusting beam angles through software. This enhances efficiency and accuracy. </p><p>In contrast, sector scanning involves physically rotating the ultrasound beam in defined sectors, allowing for detailed imaging of the material. Both types are crucial for non-destructive testing applications in various industries, ensuring structural integrity and safety.</p></p>
