<p><strong>The Marine Data Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drifting Buoy</li><li>Moored Buoy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine data buoy market comprises two primary types: drifting buoys and moored buoys. Drifting buoys are free-floating devices that move with ocean currents, collecting data on temperature, salinity, and currents over large areas. In contrast, moored buoys are anchored to a fixed position, providing continuous monitoring of specific locations, making them ideal for long-term studies. Both types play crucial roles in marine research, climate monitoring, and weather forecasting, contributing valuable data for understanding oceanic conditions.</p></p>
