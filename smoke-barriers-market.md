<p><strong>The Smoke Barriers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ActiveType</li><li>PassiveType</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smoke barriers are essential components in building safety, designed to prevent the spread of smoke and fire. The market for smoke barriers is divided into Active and Passive types. Active smoke barriers use mechanical systems like fans and dampers to control smoke movement, often integrated with fire alarms. Passive smoke barriers rely on physical structures like walls, doors, and seals to contain smoke without mechanical intervention. Both types aim to enhance safety, with active systems offering dynamic control and passive systems providing static, durable protection.</p></p>
