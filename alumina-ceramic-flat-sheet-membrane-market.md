<p><p>The Alumina Ceramic Flat Sheet Membrane market is primarily divided into two types: microfiltration and ultrafiltration. Microfiltration membranes have larger pore sizes, typically filtering out suspended solids, bacteria, and larger molecules, making them suitable for pre-treatment applications. Ultrafiltration membranes have smaller pores, allowing them to remove finer particles, such as proteins and viruses, which makes them ideal for applications in water purification and food processing. Both types leverage the durability and chemical resistance of alumina ceramics, ensuring high filtration efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
