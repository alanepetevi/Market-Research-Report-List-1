<p><strong>The Aircraft Landing Gear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strut Landing Gear</li><li>Rocker Landing Gear</li><li>Pontoon Landing Gear</li><li>Framed Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft landing gear market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Strut Landing Gear** features cylindrical struts that absorb shock during landing, commonly used in small planes. </p><p>2. **Rocker Landing Gear** utilizes a pivoting mechanism for better stability on uneven surfaces, often found in utility aircraft. </p><p>3. **Pontoon Landing Gear** is designed for amphibious aircraft, allowing water landings with buoyant floats. </p><p>4. **Framed Landing Gear** consists of a robust frame structure providing high durability, typically seen in military and cargo planes. Each type serves specific operational needs and environments.</p></p>
