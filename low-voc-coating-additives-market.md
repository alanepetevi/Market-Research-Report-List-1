<p><p>The Low-VOC Coating Additives Market comprises several types that enhance the performance of coatings while minimizing volatile organic compounds. Rheology modifiers adjust the flow and viscosity, improving application properties. Defoamers eliminate air bubbles, ensuring a smooth finish. Dispersants aid in evenly distributing pigments, preventing clumping. Wetting agents enhance surface interaction for better adhesion. Other additives may include biocides and adhesion promoters, further optimizing coatings for durability and environmental compliance while maintaining low VOC emissions.</p></p>
