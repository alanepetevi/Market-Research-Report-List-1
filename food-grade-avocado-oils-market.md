<p><p>The food-grade avocado oil market comprises three main types: Extra Virgin Avocado Oil, Refined Avocado Oil, and Virgin Avocado Oil. Extra Virgin Avocado Oil is cold-pressed and retains the fruit's natural flavor and nutrients, making it ideal for dressings and finishing dishes. Refined Avocado Oil undergoes processing to remove impurities, resulting in a higher smoke point and neutral taste, suitable for high-heat cooking. Virgin Avocado Oil is also cold-pressed but may have a slightly less intense flavor than the extra virgin variant, offering versatility in culinary uses.</p></p>
