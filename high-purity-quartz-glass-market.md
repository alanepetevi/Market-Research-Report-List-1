<p><p>The High Purity Quartz Glass market consists of two primary types: transparent quartz glass and opaque quartz glass. Transparent quartz glass is characterized by its clarity and low impurity levels, making it ideal for applications in optics, electronics, and semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, opaque quartz glass, which contains higher levels of impurities, is primarily used for applications requiring high thermal resistance and durability, such as furnace components and laboratory equipment. Both types serve distinct sectors within the high-tech and industrial markets.</p></p>
