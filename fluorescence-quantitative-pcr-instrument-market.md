<p><p>Fluorescence Quantitative PCR instruments are categorized into single-channel, dual-channel, and multi-channel types based on their detection capabilities. Single-channel instruments measure one fluorescent signal at a time, ideal for simpler assays. Dual-channel systems can simultaneously detect two different fluorophores, enhancing flexibility for multiplexing. Multi-channel instruments support multiple signals, allowing for comprehensive analysis in complex experiments or high-throughput screenings. Each type caters to varying research needs, from basic applications to advanced studies requiring extensive data collection.</p></p>
