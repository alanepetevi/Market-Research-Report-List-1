<p><strong>The Copper Heat Sink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Heat Sink</li><li>Active Heat Sink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper heat sink market is primarily divided into two types: passive and active heat sinks. Passive heat sinks rely solely on natural convection to dissipate heat, utilizing the high thermal conductivity of copper to efficiently transfer heat away from electronic components without additional power consumption. In contrast, active heat sinks incorporate mechanical devices, such as fans or pumps, to enhance heat dissipation actively. Both types are essential in thermal management, optimizing performance in various electronics applications from consumer devices to industrial machinery.</p></p>
