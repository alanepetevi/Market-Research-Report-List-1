<p><p>A pneumatic strapping tool is an industrial device used for securing packages with strapping material, typically polyolefin or polyester, using compressed air as its power source. These tools enhance efficiency by automating the strapping process, offering fast, reliable, and consistent tensioning and sealing capabilities. Commonly used in manufacturing, packaging, and distribution sectors, pneumatic strapping tools are favored for their ability to handle a variety of package sizes and shapes, ensuring optimal load stability.</p><p>The Pneumatic Strapping Tool Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period.
