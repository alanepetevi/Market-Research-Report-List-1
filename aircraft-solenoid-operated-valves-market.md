<p><p>The aircraft solenoid operated valves market comprises various types, primarily categorized as two-way and three-way solenoid valves, along with other specialized variants. Two-way solenoid valves control the flow of fluid in one line, either allowing or blocking it, making them ideal for simple on-off applications. Three-way solenoid valves manage the flow between two outlets, offering versatile control for multiple operations. Other types may include proportional valves and multi-port valves, enhancing functionality across diverse aerospace applications, including fuel management and hydraulic systems.</p></p>
