<p><strong>The Ground-source Heat Pump System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Underground Tube Ground Source Heat Pump System</li><li>Groundwater Source Heat Pump System</li><li>Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pump System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ground-source heat pump (GSHP) market comprises three main types. The Underground Tube GSHP System utilizes buried pipes to exchange heat with the ground for heating and cooling. The Groundwater Source GSHP System taps into underground water resources to transfer thermal energy, offering efficient heating and cooling. The Surface Water Ground Source GSHP System employs nearby lakes, rivers, or ponds, using water as a heat exchange medium. Each type leverages natural resources for energy efficiency, reducing reliance on conventional energy sources.</p></p>
