<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Diamond Compound</li><li>Oil-soluble Diamond Compound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond compound and paste market is categorized into two main types: water-soluble and oil-soluble diamond compounds. Water-soluble diamond compounds are designed to be mixed with water, making them easy to clean and environmentally friendly, ideal for applications requiring efficient cooling and lubrication. In contrast, oil-soluble diamond compounds are mixed with oils, providing a thicker consistency and enhanced lubrication, suitable for high-precision cutting and grinding tasks. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, including machining and surface finishing.</p></p>
